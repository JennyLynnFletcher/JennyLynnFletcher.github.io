FridgeSpace

FridgeSpace is an innovative new social networking app to connect people who have too much expiring food with others who need more. We hope to create social good by reducing food waste and encouraging people to experiment with new ingredients and cooking techniques they haven't tried before. We hope encouraging people to plan meals and share food not only helps us to be more environmentally concious but also to be able to save money and be more aware of food spending.

  • Learn more

Tracking What is in Your Fridge

FridgeSpace allows you to scan in the barcodes of any food you put in you fridge, freezer or cupboards, and will automatically look up information about the item which is added to your app. It will show you what items are about to go out of date and choose to share any food you don't think you are able to finish eating before its expiry. You can share food by joining a 'bubble' of your friends, family or local community, and anything you don't want is offered to everyone else in your bubble!

Going into the future this can be expanded by making the app smarter, for example rather than just finding out the type and weight of your food it could help you track the macro and micro-nutrients of the foods you are buying, encouraging you to eat healthier. Also further computer vision could be used to detect and automatically input expiry dates, speeding up the process of entering foods into the app.

Reducing Food Waste

Especially when living alone it can be difficult to have a varied and exciting diet without any food wastage. We hope that the app we provide here can both help you track what in your fridge next needs eating, by easily displaying the expiry dates of what's in your fridge without you even having to go near your kitchen as well as allowing you to pass on anything you are unable to eat.

Spending Less

FridgeSpace also gives you the opportunity to save money by both helping you to track what you have been buying, and therefore make more informed choices when shopping. You can also save your money by taking advantage of food shared via this app!
